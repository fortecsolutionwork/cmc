CMC Global 2021 is a two-day online conference that aims at equipping Chinese churches and Christians to produce disciple-makers and activate God’s global mission in their communities and beyond.

This conference is designed for Chinese churches, ministries, and people of all generations including families, singles, youth and empty-nesters. From dynamic speakers and creative programming to fun interactive activities, there’s something to help everyone in your church form a heart for missions.

CMC Global 2021 is a convenient and effective platform to gather with like-minded believers and connect with other Chinese churches. You’ll have the opportunity to hear in-depth teachings and panel discussions on relevant topics by dynamic speakers. You’ll also get to participate in workshops and small groups.

This conference will get your heart pumped up for missions, so we encourage you to join us!
